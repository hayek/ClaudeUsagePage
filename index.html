Frequently Asked Questions

What is Usage for Claude?

Usage for Claude is a macOS menu bar application that displays your Claude usage statistics with automatic background updates. It authenticates with claude.ai and extracts usage data directly from your account, providing convenient access to your session and weekly usage limits without opening a browser.

Is this an official Anthropic application?

No, this is an independent third-party application built by an enthusiastic Claude user out of love for the product and to help the Claude user community. It is not created, endorsed, or maintained by Anthropic.

What accounts are supported?

The app works with any Claude account (Free or Pro) that has access to the usage page at claude.ai/settings/usage.

Does the app store my password?

No. Authentication is handled entirely through Claude's official website. The app only preserves session cookies to maintain your login state—your password is never stored.

Is there an iOS or iPad app?

Yes! Usage for Claude is available as a free companion app for iPhone and iPad on the App Store. The companion apps display your usage history, charts, and insights synced from the macOS app—they do not track usage by themselves and require the Mac app to fetch data.

How does iCloud sync work?

Your usage history automatically syncs across all your devices via iCloud. The macOS app collects the data, and it's shared with the iOS companion app in real-time. Both devices must be signed into the same iCloud account. Your data stays private in your personal iCloud storage and is never shared with third parties.

Is my data shared with anyone?

No. All usage data remains on your local Mac. The app includes anonymous analytics to help improve the experience, but this is never connected to your identity or Claude account.

Does it include a widget?

Yes, the app includes a macOS widget that can be added to Notification Center or your desktop, displaying the same usage information as the menu bar popover.

What is the History view?

The History view displays your Claude usage over time with interactive charts, a GitHub-style activity grid showing your usage patterns over the past year, and detailed insights about your usage habits. Access it from the menu bar by clicking 'History'.

Can I share my usage statistics?

Yes, click the share button in the History view to generate a shareable image of your usage statistics. The image includes your usage chart, activity grid, and key insights. You can share it on social media, messaging apps, or save it to your device.

What are the usage predictions?

Predictions analyze your current usage rate to estimate when you might hit your session or weekly limits. If you're on track to stay within limits, you'll see an 'On Track' message. If you're using Claude heavily, you'll see a warning with an estimated time until you hit the limit.

Will I receive notifications?

Yes, the app sends smart notifications when it predicts you're on pace to hit your session or weekly limit, based on your current usage rate. You can manage notification preferences in System Settings.

Is the app free?

Yes, Usage for Claude is completely free with no in-app purchases or subscriptions.

What are the system requirements?

macOS 15.0 (Sequoia) or later, compatible with both Intel and Apple Silicon Macs. An internet connection is required for fetching usage data.
